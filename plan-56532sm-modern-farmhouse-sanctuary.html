Concept direction

This overlay is an elevated farmhouse - warm, grounded, minimal, and timeless. No trend overload. We focus on cohesion: finishes, lighting layers, storage logic, and flow.

Palette (Luxora Sanctuary)

  • Warm off-white walls (soft mineral / limewash feel)
  • Mushroom greige accents + quiet contrast
  • Natural oak (matte) for warmth + longevity
  • Aged brass hardware and fixtures
  • Soft black accents (windows, rail, select lighting)
  • Optional muted sage / dusty olive for depth

Exterior overlay

A clean modern farmhouse with quiet luxury - not stark, not harsh.

  • Warm white board + batten (not bright white)
  • Cedar porch posts or soffit warmth layer
  • Soft black window trim + accents
  • Wood door with vertical grain
  • Light limestone tone stone skirt / foundation accent
  • Upgrade: standing seam metal roof at porch only

The Luxora wellness layer

  • Nervous-system zoning: "quiet retreat" vs "family flow" areas
  • Lighting temperature guidance by room and time of day
  • Sound-softening materials and layout recommendations
  • Declutter-first storage logic (so calm is sustainable)

What you get
